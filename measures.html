Measures

Focus on SDG-Goal 6 related measures and other easy-to-measure variables important for public and animal health and local economy.

General water quantity and quality

  • Water level (community)
  • Water clarity (community)
  • Dissolved oxygen — real-time automated sensors (CEH)
  • Nutrients and chl-a — (Indian partners)
  • Water colour — smartphone app "Eye on the Water"
  • Quality of groundwater recharge [HNS PhD]

Ecological health

  • Plant richness and cover (SCM) — CEH and Indian staff/students
  • Invertebrate diversity —CEH and Indian staff/students
  • Fish species and size-structure — fishermen log book

Economic indicators — who can collect these?

  • Fish Catch per Unit Effort — fishermen log book
  • Other related job creation and income from pond maintenance, fishery, pond/wetland crops, water supply to irrigation, etc. [HNS PhD]

Health indicators

  • Human Pathogens: Faecal Indicator Organisms (FIOs) — NIH Roorkee (MK Sharma?)
  • Human Pathogens: DNA-base bacteria identities — CEH (Dan Read)
  • Mosquito larvae (1 min kick sample) — Relate to vector-borne disease (malaria)
  • Harmful algal blooms (cyanobacteria) — smartphone app "Bloomin' Algae"
