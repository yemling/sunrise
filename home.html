Sustainable use of natural resources to improve human health and support economic development (SUNRISE)

Our aim is to develop a long-term collaboratve research programme addressing India-UK science priorities and UN SDGs: July 2017 — June 2020

Three key themes:

Advancement of hydro-climate services

  1. flood and drought risk assessment and forecasting
  2. water resources assessment [Gwyn Rees]

Restoration and remediation of degraded environments and resources

  1. remediation of soils
  2. restoration of urban air quality
  3. enhancing freshwater resources
  4. vector-borne pathogens of humans and livestock

Integrated assessment and management of natural capital

  1. Natural capital assessment
  2. Sustainable agriculture and agroforestry
