Study sites

Three treatment and three nearby control ponds as a minimum experimental design (six sites total).

Four rural sites in the Upper Ganga catchment coordinated as part of NIH Roorkee's government-funded rural village pond improvement project in the Ibrahimpur-Masahi village region.

Two urban sites part of ATREE's community programmes to manage and improve water resources in the city of Bangalore, e.g. Jakkur and Bellandur lakes.
